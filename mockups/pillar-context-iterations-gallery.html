Peek card & filmstrip iterations

You liked V1 (peek card) and V4 (filmstrip). Here are three refinements of each — same copy, different chrome and interaction.

Peek card family

V1 · Original

Peek card

Top-right floating card · auto-rotate · expand / minimize tab · promise strip.

 Peek B

Slide drawer

Peeks slide in from the right edge. Progress bar on rotation. Collapse to vertical tab.

 Peek C

Bloom pill

Bottom-left pulsing pill blooms into card. Trigger label shows active highlight title.

 Peek D

Timer bar

Inline bar under promise — no overlay. Animated timer + prev/next. Always in layout flow.

Filmstrip family

V4 · Original

Auto filmstrip

Scrolling cards at zone bottom · hover pauses · click lifts detail panel.

 Filmstrip B

Hover dock

Slim handle until hover or pin. Cards expand to show full body on reveal.

 Filmstrip C

Focus carousel

Center card grows with full copy; neighbors stay compact. Auto-advances.

 Filmstrip D

Ticker + lift

Ultra-thin marquee line · minimal footprint · click lifts detail above strip.

Tip: Peek D + Filmstrip D pair well — inline peek up top, ticker at bottom, graph stays clean in the middle.
