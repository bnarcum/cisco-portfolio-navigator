What this mockup proves

  • mesh3d-style editorial flow fits CPN without replacing Overview or Spatial
  • Scroll-snapped sections: hero → stats → 3D wall → picks → pillars → CTA
  • Exhibit click maps to family detail panel (same as graph selection)
  • Real build: Three.js planes + product photos, reuse Spatial texture pipeline
  • Mobile fallback: stacked 2D cards instead of perspective wall
